below, we will talk about the two types of diabetes mellitus.

1- Diabetes type 1

Definition:

it is a chronic disease characterized by high blood glucose levels due to autoimmune reaction result in low insulin level.

Causes:

  • immune system mistakenly attacks the insulin-producing cells in your pancreas.
  • family history.

Symptoms:

  • excess thirst.
  • extreme hunger.
  • weight loss.
  • slowed healing of cuts and sores.

Treatment:

  • taking insulin.
  • monitoring blood glucose levels.
  • eating healthy food
  • doing exercise

2- Diabetes type 2

Definition:

it is a chronic disease charactarized by high blood glucose levels due to insulin resistance.

Causes:

  • insulin resistance.
  • family history.

Symptoms:

  • excess thirst.
  • extreme hunger.
  • weight loss.
  • slowed healing of cuts.
  • numbness in hand or feet.

Treatment:

  • insulin therapy if needed.
  • eating healthy food
  • doing excercise
