Obesity

Definition:

it is a medical disease characterized by high body mass index (30 or more) or excessive body fat.

Causes:

  • excessive calorie intake and lack of physical activity.
  • family history.
  • unhealthy diet.
  • stress.

Symptoms:

  • BMI= 30 or more.
  • shortness of breath.
  • unusual sweating.

Treatment:

  • surgery.
  • diet.
  • eating healthy food
  • doing exercise
