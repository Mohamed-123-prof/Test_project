Definition: Cancer is a group of diseases involving uncontrolled cell growth resulting in tumors with the potential to invade or spread to other parts of the body.

Below, we will talk about the most common types found in Egypt.

1- Lung cancer

Definition:

abnormal growth of lung cells and it may be fatal.

Causes:

  • the primary cause is smoking.
  • exposure to secondhand smoke.
  • exposure to radon gas.
  • previous radiation therapy.
  • family history.

Symptoms:

Early signs:

  • chest pain.
  • chronic cough.
  • trouble breathing.
  • weight loss.

Advanced symptoms:

  • pain in the bones of the back or hips.
  • swollen lymph nodes of the neck or collarbone region.
  • headache, dizziness, balance issues, seizures, weakness in the limbs and NS problems.
  • blood clots.
  • liver problems.

Treatment:

  • surgery.
  • radiation therapy.
  • chemotherapy.
  • immunotherapy.
  • targeted therapy.

2- breast cancer

Definition:

abnormal growth of breast cells , it is mainly in women, but can be in men.

Causes:

The exact causes are unknown, but there are factors increase chance of breast cancer.

  • lifestyle.
  • family history.
  • hormones.
  • alcohol consumption

Symptoms:

  • breast lump.
  • change in color of breast skin.
  • change in size, shape or appearance of breast.
  • Peeling, scaling, crusting or flaking of the skin on the breast.

Treatment:

  • surgery.
  • chemotherapy.
  • radiation therapy.
  • hormone therapy.

3- liver cancer

Definition:

abnormal growth of liver cells.

Causes:

  • effects of HBV (hepatitis B virus).
  • effects of HCV (hepatitis C virus).
  • non-alcoholic fatty liver disease
  • diabetes or obesity.
  • family history.

Symptoms:

  • jaundice.
  • upper abdominal pain and/or abdominal swelling.
  • General weakness and fatigue.
  • Nausea and vomiting.

Treatment:

  • surgery.
  • chemotherapy.
  • radiation therapy.
  • targeted therapy.
